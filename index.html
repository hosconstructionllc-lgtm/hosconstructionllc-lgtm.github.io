Who We Are

HOS Construction, LLC is a premier commercial and residential construction company based in Houston, Texas. We specialize in delivering high-quality construction projects throughout the Greater Houston area.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and client-focused approach has established us as a trusted partner for construction projects of all sizes, from custom homes to large-scale commercial developments.

From initial concept to final completion, we work closely with our clients to ensure every project exceeds expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.
